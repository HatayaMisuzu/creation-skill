# Character Authoring Checklist

Use this before delivering a new or updated `CHARACTER.md`.

## Before Writing

- The task type is clear: new card, update, repair, project, export, or simulation.
- The project/work and character version are identified.
- Source confirmation is complete, or unconfirmed sources are kept out of evidence.
- User relationship defaults are known, or default to familiar but not intimate.
- Evidence notes distinguish target speech, other evaluation, context, fan analysis, user settings, and simulation.

## While Writing

- Keep the card runnable by a generic agent.
- Put operational rules in direct language.
- Mark sparse evidence as sparse.
- Preserve the user's language rule.
- Keep relationship network internal.
- Keep safety mode explicit.
- Keep self-learning separate from canon.
- Do not write long encyclopedia summaries where behavior rules are needed.

## After Writing

- Run 3-5 internal audition turns.
- Fix language mismatch, profile recitation, generic voice, meta leakage, relationship over-expansion, or weak refusal.
- Check frontmatter and sections.
- Check no placeholder or mojibake remains.
- Summarize risks and recommended next materials for the user.
